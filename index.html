San Francisco Silenced Its Sirens

In 2019, the City shut off its emergency alert sirens. Years later, they remain dark — and no plan exists to bring them back. We believe that's a public safety failure. It’s time to restore a system that alerts everyone — in every neighborhood, in every language, with one simple sound.

Sign the Petition

Why Sirens Still Matter

  • Universal: Sirens reach people with no phone, no internet, no app.
  • Resilient: They work when cell networks crash during emergencies.
  • Equitable: One sound alerts everyone — no matter what language they speak.
