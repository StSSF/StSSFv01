Why We Exist

Sound the Siren SF is a grassroots campaign to restore and modernize San Francisco’s outdoor emergency warning system. Our mission is to ensure that all residents — regardless of language, wealth, or connectivity — can be alerted in the event of a disaster.

What We're Working Toward

  • Reinstating and upgrading the city's public warning sirens
  • Raising public awareness and support for emergency alert systems
  • Partnering with city officials and agencies to advance solutions

Who We Are

We are volunteers, public safety advocates, and San Franciscans who care deeply about community resilience and equitable access to emergency information.
